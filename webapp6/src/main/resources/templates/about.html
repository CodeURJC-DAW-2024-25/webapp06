About Us

GlobalMart is a platform that allows you to buy and sell products. We offer a wide range of products from electronics to fashion, home, and books. Our goal is to provide a seamless shopping experience for our customers.

We are Luis, Juanma and Cristian, the founders of GlobalMart. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, We have dedicated our career to creating a unique shopping experience for our customers. Our journey began with a simple idea: to provide high-quality products that cater to the diverse needs of our customers. Over the years, we have worked tirelessly to bring this vision to life, and we are proud of the progress we have made.

Outside of work, we enjoy playing padel and basketball, which help us stay inspired and motivated. We believe in the power of community and strive to make a positive impact through our work.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless shopping experience for our customers. We are committed to delivering high-quality products at affordable prices. We strive to create a platform that caters to the diverse needs of our customers and provides them with a wide range of products to choose from. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent customer service and ensuring that our customers have a great shopping experience.
